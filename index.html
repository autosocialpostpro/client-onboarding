Before you continue

You need at least one social media account

To use AutoSocialPostPro, you need at least one of the following set up and ready. The good news — they are all free to create.

📘 Facebook Business Page

A Facebook Business Page is separate from your personal profile. It lets customers find and follow your business.

How to create one:

1. Go to facebook.com/pages/create
2. Choose "Business or Brand"
3. Fill in your business name and category
4. Add a profile photo and cover image
5. Your page is live — come back and fill this form

📸 Instagram Business Account

An Instagram Business account gives you access to insights and allows us to post on your behalf automatically.

How to set it up:

1. Open Instagram on your phone
2. Go to Settings → Account → Switch to Professional Account
3. Choose "Business"
4. Connect to your Facebook Page
5. Done — come back and fill this form

🗺️ Google Business Profile

Google Business Profile puts your business on Google Maps and local search results. It is one of the most powerful free tools for local businesses.

How to create one:

1. Go to business.google.com
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Enter your business name and address
4. Google will send a verification postcard — this takes 1–2 weeks
5. Once verified, come back and fill this form

Once you have set up at least one account, come back and continue your onboarding.
